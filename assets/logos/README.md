# Party Logos

This directory contains party logos for Tamil Nadu political parties.

## Logo Sources

Based on research, here are the recommended sources for downloading party logos:

### DMK (Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam)
- Symbol: Rising Sun
- Download from: https://seeklogo.com/vector-logo/274826/dmk
- Format: PNG or SVG

### AIADMK (All India Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam)
- Symbol: Two Leaves
- Download from: https://seeklogo.com/vector-logo/274827/aiadmk
- Alternative: Wikimedia Commons - "AIADMK OfficialFlag Vector.svg"
- Format: PNG or SVG

### BJP (Bharatiya Janata Party)
- Symbol: Lotus
- Download from: Official BJP website or logo repositories
- Format: PNG or SVG

### INC (Indian National Congress)
- Symbol: Hand
- Download from: Official Congress website or logo repositories
- Format: PNG or SVG

### PMK (Pattali Makkal Katchi)
- Symbol: Mango
- Download from: Wikimedia Commons - "PMK.svg"
- Format: PNG or SVG

### Other Parties
- MDMK, VCK, CPI, CPI(M), DMDK, etc.
- Search on Wikimedia Commons or party websites

## Instructions

1. Download logos from the sources above
2. Save them in this directory with the following naming convention:
   - `dmk.png` or `dmk.svg`
   - `aiadmk.png` or `aiadmk.svg`
   - `bjp.png` or `bjp.svg`
   - etc.
3. Recommended size: 32x32 pixels or larger (will be scaled down to 14x14 in the UI)
4. Recommended format: PNG with transparent background or SVG

## Fallback

If a logo is not found, the image will be hidden automatically (handled by `onerror="this.style.display='none'"` in the HTML).
